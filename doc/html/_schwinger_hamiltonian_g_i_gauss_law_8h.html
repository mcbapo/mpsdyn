Implementation of the Schwinger Hamiltonian in the spin representation with simplified gauge fields that violate the gauge invariance of the Hamiltonian By contrast to the other version the exponential for the link fields is calculted exactly which renders this version "more gauge invariant" List of inital states that can be automatically generated, |u> means spin up, |d> spin down |i> with integer i gives the flux between two spins
